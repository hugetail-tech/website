This policy does not apply to any information collected offline or outside of this app, nor is it suitable for any interaction you make with us directly through social media or email.

Information we collect

1. CanGoal does not collect any personal information from you. Feel free to use our app without concern.
2. CanGoal does not request any personal information from you, including but not limited to your name, gender, email address, phone number, or address.

(However, this will not affect your purchases and subscriptions.)
